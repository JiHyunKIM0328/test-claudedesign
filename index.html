01 / About

A product designer who listens before drawing.

{\\ bio}

I'm Harbor — a product & UI/UX designer working primarily in fintech and e-learning. I care about the interfaces people use every day: the ones that handle their money, their attention, their time.

My work is built on research, systems thinking, and a quiet editorial sensibility. I believe the best tools disappear — until you need them.

I've shipped work with early-stage startups and established platforms, led end-to-end flows from discovery through handoff, and maintained design systems that grew with the team.
